I can help you:

Plan Your Strategy & Story

Who are you? What are your most important activities? What are you passionate about? It can be tough to make sense of your own high school journey. My goal here is to help you decide on the activities that will form the personalized story you share with colleges.

Edit Your Essays for Clarity & Strength

Often, quality students struggle to express themselves or package their experiences into a coherent narrative. I'll help you brainstorm and edit your personal statement and secondaries, and I'll guide you as you form stories that are clear and compelling. My goal here is to help you learn how to present yourself to colleges!

You will get the most out of my help if you are:

  • A senior in high school. If you are a sophomore/junior, we can schedule a meeting where I can offer advice on choosing strong extracurricular activities, figuring out summer programs, and planning for the future.
  • Planning to apply to University of California (UC) schools or out-of-state private schools like Yale, Penn, Cornell, NYU, etc.
  • Eager to grow, passionate about things, and ambitious!

Let me see how I can help you!

Due to time constraints, I might not be able to take all students who need help, but I highly recommend that you reach out to me by email. Please include the following in your email:

  1. Your year and high school name.
  2. Your target school list (which UCs, private schools, states, etc.).
  3. Two to three 30-min time slots (day and time) when you are available for a free "get-to-know-you" call.

Pricing is affordable and will depend on several factors: we'll work it out over a call.
